BBQ Scraper

Research

Step 1: Research

First, I did some research, looking at existing barbecue scraper designs from the internet, and then noting the ones that I liked and the specific aspects that I wanted for my own with thumbnail sketches (small, quick, sketches). Many scrapers had the same general shape, but they all had different dimensions and sizes, or small things in their designs that made them unique, such as finger grooves or different neck/handle/blade shapes. However, some were completely different, like a hole in the handle, or the lack of a neck (the whole thing was pretty much a blade). I later combined the individual parts I liked to create my own design for my scraper. Below are some images of designs that I liked, as well as my thumbnail sketches.
