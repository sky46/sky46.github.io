About Me


I'm a student in Grade 9 at Merivale High School. Science is my favourite subject. I play the guitar and love listening to music. I also enjoy playing hockey, tennis and ultimate frisbee. I've lived in Riverside South for most of my life. Through kindergarten, elementary, and middle school, I went to Steve Maclean Public School (that's 10 years!).


In my free time, I like playing video games. My favourites are Minecraft (Java Edition), The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, and Mario Kart Wii. Lately, I've also been playing some Valorant, League of Legends, Hollow Knight, and Stardew Valley. I play on the Nintendo Switch and on my PC (i.e. old slow laptop).


I love music! I've been learning to play guitar for around 2 years now with a local teacher named Lucas Haneman. He has a Blues band called the Lucas Haneman Express - Check them out here! Their music is on YouTube and Spotify. Honestly, I have to admit that I haven't listened to that much music. Mostly just specific songs or bands that I like or hear on the radio (though these days I mostly listen to Spotify). My favourite band is Radiohead, and my favourite song is No Surprises (by Radiohead). I also listen to John Mayer, The Beatles, Ed Sheeran, and quite a few others.


I love using technology. Playing video games and programming are some of my favourite hobbies. I started taking CS50x in late 2020, and it was a really great experience. I had a bit of prior experience from learning Python from Python Crash Course by Eric Matthes, but CS50 still taught me a lot. In the CS50 community, I've met many passionate, skilled programmers. Now, I'm taking CS50's Web Programming Course. I love programming because it's amazing seeing code that you wrote put into action. Technology is almost magical sometimes!
