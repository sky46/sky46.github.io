I love music!

I play the guitar and (try to) sing a bit too. I also listen to music a lot.

I am the guitarist in the Nepean All-City Lab Band as well as in the Merivale High School Jazz Band.

Featured Recordings

My favourite song is No Surprises, off OK Computer, my favourite album, by Radiohead, my favourite band. I mostly listen to pop and some rock. Some other artists I like are John Mayer, Ed Sheeran, The Beatles, Queen, Taylor Swift, Nothing But Thieves, Blur, Scott Helman, Weezer, John Scofield, Julian Lage, Pat Metheny, and Wes Montgomery.

I started taking guitar lessons in spring of 2019 with a local musician named Lucas Haneman. He plays guitar and sings in a blues band called The Lucas Haneman Express. Give them a listen on Spotify, SoundCloud, or YouTube!

I also played piano for some years before I started guitar. Though I wasn't very good, it definitely helped me improve my ear, rhythm, and music theory knowledge.
